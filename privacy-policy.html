Introduction

Decision Coin ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Decision Coin is designed with privacy in mind. The app:

  • Does not require account creation or login
  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not display advertisements

Data Storage

The only data stored by the app is your flip history (last 10 coin flips), which is stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not shared with any third party
  • Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

Decision Coin does not use any third-party services that collect data.

Children's Privacy

Decision Coin does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.
