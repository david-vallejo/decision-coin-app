Make decisions instantly with Decision Coin - the elegant coin flip app that helps you choose when you can't decide.

Whether you're settling a friendly debate, picking where to eat, or making any either/or choice, Decision Coin delivers a fair, random result with satisfying animations and haptic feedback.

FEATURES:

- Classic Heads/Tails Mode - Traditional coin flip with beautiful 3D animation
- Custom A/B Mode - Enter your own options (up to 150 characters each) for personalized decisions
- Gesture Controls - Flick the coin upward to flip, or tap the button
- Shake to Reset - Simply shake your device to clear the result
- Flip History - View your last 10 flips to track decisions
- Haptic Feedback - Feel the flip with tactile responses
- Clean Design - Minimalist interface that's easy to use

PERFECT FOR:

- Settling debates fairly
- Choosing between two options
- Making quick everyday decisions
- Games and activities
- When you just can't decide

Decision Coin respects your privacy - no data collection, no ads, no accounts required. Just flip and decide.
