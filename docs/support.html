How to Use the App

Flip a Coin: Tap the "Flip Coin" button or flick up on the coin to flip it.

Custom Mode: Switch to "Custom (A/B)" mode to enter your own options for each side of the coin.

View History: Tap "History" to see your last 10 flips.

Reset: Shake your device to reset the coin.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the coin flip truly random?

Yes! Each flip uses a cryptographically random number generator to ensure a fair 50/50 outcome.

Is my data stored anywhere?

Your flip history is stored only on your device. We don't collect or store any of your data on our servers.

How do I clear my history?

Open the History panel and tap "Clear History" at the bottom.
